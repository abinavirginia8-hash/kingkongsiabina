The Badminton Team is SEPARATE from the general club. You do not have to be a competitive player to join the club -- however, the team is highly selective! The Team has about 16 members (8 men, 8 women) and represents Jade University at intercollegiate competitions. The Columbia Team has a respectable track record and won the most recent Eastern Intercollegiate. Watch our vlogs for more!
